Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping at PawHaus

How long does shipping take?

Is shipping free?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over S$50 within Singapore. Orders under S$50 have a flat shipping fee of S$4.90.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure Stripe-powered checkout. All prices are in Singapore Dollars (SGD).
Can I return or exchange a product?
Absolutely! We offer a 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll arrange a return or exchange. Items must be unused and in original packaging.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also contact us at hello@pawhaus.com with your order number.
Are your products safe for pets?
Yes! All products in our store are made from pet-safe, non-toxic materials. We carefully vet every item before adding it to our catalogue to ensure it meets quality and safety standards.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within Singapore. We're working on expanding to Malaysia and other Southeast Asian countries soon. Stay tuned!
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach us via email at hello@pawhaus.com or WhatsApp at +65 9123 4567. We typically respond within 24 hours.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
If your order hasn't shipped yet, we can cancel or modify it. Please contact us as soon as possible with your order number. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled but you can return it under our 30-day return policy.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?
Yes! If you're a pet shop, groomer, or buying in bulk, contact us at hello@pawhaus.com for special pricing.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
